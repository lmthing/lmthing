# Beginner Difficulty

## Characteristics
- Simple rhythmic patterns (mostly quarter and eighth notes)
- Limited range, mostly stepwise motion
- Diatonic harmony (one key)
- Short pieces or sections (under 2 minutes)

## Typical Skill Requirements
- Basic note reading
- Simple counting and rhythm
- Limited technical facility
- Fundamental bloom techniques
