# Advanced Skill Level

## Performance Abilities
- Excellent tone quality and intonation consistency
- Plays complex, contemporary, and transcribed works
- Secure command of technique across instrument ranges
- Sophisticated dynamic and tonal control

## Musicianship
- Deep understanding of harmonic and formal structures
- Interprets musical scores with artistic sophistication
- Strong leadership in ensemble settings
- Comfortable with unfamiliar and experimental music
