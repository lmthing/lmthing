# Fried (Τηγανητά)

Greeks love fried food — from fish to cheese to vegetables.

## Technique Basics
- Use olive oil (traditional)
- Moderate temperature (not smoking)
- Don't overcrowd the pan
- Drain on paper towels
- Serve immediately

## Pan-Fried Dishes
- Small fish (marides, barbounia)
- Saganaki (fried cheese)
- Keftedakia (mini meatballs)
- Tigania (pork pan-fried with wine)
- Eggs (Greek-style with olive oil)

## Deep-Fried Dishes
- Kalamari (squid rings)
- Bakaliaros (cod)
- Kolokithokeftedes (zucchini fritters)
- Tyropitakia (cheese triangles)
- Loukoumades (honey puffs)

## Flour Coating
For fish and vegetables:
- Light flour dusting
- No batter typically
- Seasoned flour (salt, pepper)
- Shake off excess

## The Greek Touch
- Serve with lemon wedges always
- Skordalia (garlic dip) for fish
- Tzatziki for fritters
- Hot from the pan to plate
