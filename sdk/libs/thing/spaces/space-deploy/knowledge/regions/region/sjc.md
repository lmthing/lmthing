# US West — San Jose, California (SJC)

Silicon Valley datacenter with strong West Coast and trans-Pacific connectivity.

## Why Choose SJC

- **West Coast proximity** — Lowest latency for US West Coast users
- **Asia-Pacific links** — Better trans-Pacific connectivity than East Coast regions
- **Silicon Valley** — Strong infrastructure and peering ecosystem
- **Complementary** — Good alternative when IAD doesn't match your geographic needs

## Best For

- US West Coast users and teams
- Spaces serving Asia-Pacific audiences
- Silicon Valley-based organizations
- Applications needing lower latency to Asian services

## Latency

- US West Coast: < 10ms
- US East Coast: ~60ms
- Japan/Korea: ~100-120ms
- Europe: ~140-160ms
