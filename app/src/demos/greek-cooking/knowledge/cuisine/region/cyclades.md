# Cyclades (Κυκλάδες)

The Cycladic islands (Santorini, Mykonos, Paros, Naxos) feature simple, fresh cuisine shaped by the sea and volcanic soil.

## Key Characteristics
- Fresh seafood emphasis
- Local cheeses (each island unique)
- Volcanic soil ingredients (Santorini)
- Simple preparations highlight quality
- Wind-dried fish and meats

## Signature Dishes
- Fava Santorinis (split pea purée)
- Amorgos pasteli (sesame bars)
- Sun-dried octopus
- Kopanisti (spicy cheese)
- Tomatokeftedes (tomato fritters)

## Pantry Essentials
- Cherry tomatoes (Santorini PDO)
- Capers
- Local cheese varieties
- White eggplant
- Assyrtiko wine (for cooking and drinking)
