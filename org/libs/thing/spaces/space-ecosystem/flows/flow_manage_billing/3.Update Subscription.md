Generate precise instructions for changing the user's subscription based on their desired action. Handle three scenarios:

**New subscription** — Use the create-checkout endpoint with the appropriate price_id. This creates a Stripe Checkout session and returns a URL. The user completes payment on Stripe's hosted page and is redirected back to lmthing.

**Manage existing** — Use the billing-portal endpoint to open Stripe's Customer Portal for updating payment methods, switching plans, canceling, and viewing invoices.

**Important behaviors to communicate:**
- Upgrading takes effect immediately — new models and limits are available right away
- Downgrading takes effect at the end of the current billing period
- Canceling Computer tier automatically destroys the Fly.io machine (data is lost unless synced to GitHub)
- Stripe creates the customer record lazily on first AI call or checkout

Confirm the user understands the implications before providing the action endpoint, especially for downgrades that may affect running services.
