# OpenAI Models

OpenAI provides the broadest range of models on lmthing, from fast and affordable to flagship reasoning.

## Available Models

- **openai/gpt-4o** — Flagship multimodal model. Strong at general tasks, tool calling, and coding. Good balance of speed and capability.
- **openai/gpt-4o-mini** — Smaller, faster, cheaper. Available on free tier. Excellent for everyday tasks where you don't need the full power of GPT-4o.
- **openai/gpt-4.1** — Latest generation with improved instruction following and coding. Good for agents that need precise, structured outputs.
- **openai/o3** — Reasoning model. Takes longer but produces more thoughtful, step-by-step responses. Best for complex analysis, math, and multi-step reasoning.

## Strengths

- **Tool calling** — OpenAI models have the most reliable tool/function calling support
- **Speed** — Generally fast response times, especially gpt-4o-mini
- **Broad capability** — Performs well across writing, coding, analysis, and conversation
- **Structured output** — Excellent at generating JSON, following formats, and structured responses

## When to Choose OpenAI

Choose OpenAI when you need reliable tool calling, fast responses, or broad general capability. GPT-4o-mini is the best starting point for most agents. Use GPT-4o or GPT-4.1 when you need higher quality. Use o3 for complex reasoning tasks.

## Naming Format

All OpenAI models use the `openai/` prefix: `openai/gpt-4o`, `openai/gpt-4o-mini`, etc.
