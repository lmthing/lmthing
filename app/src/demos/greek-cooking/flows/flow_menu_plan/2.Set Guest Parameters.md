# Set Guest Parameters

Plan around your guests' needs:

## Number of Guests
- **2-4 people**: Intimate, fewer dishes needed
- **6-10 people**: Standard Greek gathering
- **12+ people**: Large celebration, buffet-style

## Dietary Considerations

### Religious Fasting (Νηστεία)
Many Greeks fast before major holidays:
- No meat, dairy, or eggs
- Seafood usually permitted
- Olive oil always allowed

### Vegetarian Guests
Greek cuisine is naturally vegetarian-friendly:
- Rich tradition of ladera dishes
- Substantial cheese and egg courses
- Beans and legumes as protein

### Allergies/Restrictions
- **Nut-free**: Modify baklava, skip walnut dishes
- **Gluten-free**: Many naturally GF options
- **Dairy-free**: Focus on ladera, seafood

## Cooking Capacity
- How many burners/oven space?
- Can you prep ahead?
- Do you need help?

## Budget
- Seafood and lamb are premium
- Legume dishes are economical
- Balance across courses
