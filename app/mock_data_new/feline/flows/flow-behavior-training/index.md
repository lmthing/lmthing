# Behavior Training Plan Flow

This flow creates a customized training plan to address specific behavioral issues or teach new behaviors.

## Agent

Uses: **Behavior Trainer**

## Steps

1. Identify and analyze the behavior issue or training goal
2. Determine root causes and motivations
3. Design environmental modifications
4. Create step-by-step training protocol
5. Establish progress tracking system
6. Provide ongoing support guidelines

## Output

A detailed training plan including:
- Behavior analysis and root causes
- Environmental setup changes
- Specific training exercises with timing
- Reward strategies
- Progress milestones
- Troubleshooting tips
- Timeline expectations
