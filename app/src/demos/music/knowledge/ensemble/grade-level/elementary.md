# Elementary School

**Grade Range:** K-5 (Ages 5-11)

## Key Characteristics
- Short attention spans (10-15 minutes of focus)
- Love rhythm, movement, and singing
- Developing reading and music skills
- Prefer fun, relatable content

## Repertoire Suggestions
- Short, simple pieces with repetition
- Folk songs and traditional melody
- Pieces with actions or movement
- Age-appropriate harmonic complexity
