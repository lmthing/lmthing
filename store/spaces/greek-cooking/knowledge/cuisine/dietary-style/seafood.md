# Seafood-Focused Greek Cooking

Island and coastal Greek cuisine centering on fresh fish and seafood.

## Key Principles
- Freshness is paramount
- Simple preparations highlight quality
- Olive oil and lemon are essential
- Don't overcook fish

## Classic Seafood Dishes
- Grilled whole fish (lavraki, tsipoura)
- Octopus (grilled, stewed, with pasta)
- Calamari (fried or stuffed)
- Shrimp saganaki
- Kakavia (fisherman's soup)
- Marinated anchovies
- Mussels with ouzo

## Cooking Methods
- **Grilling**: Whole fish with olive oil and lemon
- **Plaki**: Baked in tomato sauce with vegetables
- **Saganaki**: Pan-cooked in tomato-feta sauce
- **Psito**: Oven-roasted with potatoes

## Sourcing Tips
- Whole fish over fillets
- Wild-caught when possible
- Ask your fishmonger what's fresh today
