# Beginner Skill Level

## Performance Abilities
- Can produce clear, steady tones
- Plays simple melodies with occasional errors
- Proper posture and basic technique developing
- Limited dynamic control

## Musicianship
- Understands basic rhythm concepts
- Recognizes simple musical forms
- Responds to conductor/director cues
- Building listening awareness
