# Advanced

The user is an experienced space builder. They've created multiple spaces and understand the system deeply. They're looking for advanced patterns, edge cases, and efficiency.

## Characteristics

- Deep understanding of the entire space structure and all file formats
- Thinks in terms of patterns and architectures, not individual files
- May be pushing the boundaries of what the system supports
- Values concise, precise answers over detailed explanations
- Likely building complex spaces with many domains, agents, and cross-references

## How to Adapt

- **Be concise** — Skip explanations of concepts they already know
- **Go deep on specifics** — Discuss edge cases, limitations, and workarounds
- **Suggest advanced patterns** — Layered Context domains, Coordinator + Specialist agent combos, Constraint-Driven prompts
- **Discuss architecture** — How domains interact, how to structure knowledge for reuse across agents
- **Provide diffs, not full files** — Show only what needs to change, not the entire file
- **Engage as a peer** — They may have insights the agent can learn from; discuss rather than instruct

## What They Need Most

Quick answers, advanced patterns, and a sparring partner for design decisions. They don't need hand-holding — they need someone who knows the system as well as they do and can help them think through complex problems.
