# Europe West — London, United Kingdom (LHR)

Located in London with excellent connectivity to Western Europe and transatlantic links.

## Why Choose LHR

- **European proximity** — Lowest latency for users in the UK and Western Europe
- **GDPR considerations** — Data stays in Europe for compliance-sensitive workloads
- **Strong connectivity** — London is a major internet hub with excellent peering
- **Transatlantic links** — Good connectivity to US East Coast (~80ms)

## Best For

- Users in the United Kingdom and Western Europe
- Teams with data sovereignty requirements
- European-focused applications and services
- Users who want low latency to both European and US services

## Latency

- UK/Western Europe: < 10ms
- Central Europe: ~20-30ms
- US East Coast: ~80ms
- US West Coast: ~140ms
