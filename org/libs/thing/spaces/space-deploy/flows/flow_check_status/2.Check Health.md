# Check Health

For each running space, verify health checks are passing:

- Access the health endpoint at `{fly_app_name}.fly.dev/health`
- A healthy space responds with 200 status within 5 seconds
- Health checks run automatically every 10 seconds

**Signs of trouble:**
- Health endpoint doesn't respond → machine may be crashed or overloaded
- Slow responses (>3 seconds) → approaching timeout, may indicate resource pressure
- Intermittent failures → unstable state, may need restart

For stopped spaces, health checks don't apply — the machine isn't running. For failed spaces, the machine is likely not responding at all.

Document health findings for each running space before moving to the next step.
