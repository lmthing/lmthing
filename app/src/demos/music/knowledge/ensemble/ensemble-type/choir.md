# Choir Ensemble

## Characteristics
- Vocal ensemble with multiple voice parts (SATB: soprano, alto, tenor, bass)
- Flexible size from small chamber choirs to large choral groups
- Focus on blend, balance, and intonation

## Rehearsal Considerations
- Warm-up and vocal technique development essential
- Equal attention to all voice parts for balance
- Extensive use of solfege and ear training
- Large body of choral literature available
