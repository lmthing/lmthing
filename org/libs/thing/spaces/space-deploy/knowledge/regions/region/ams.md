# Europe Central — Amsterdam, Netherlands (AMS)

Amsterdam datacenter with exceptional internet exchange peering and central European positioning.

## Why Choose AMS

- **Internet exchange hub** — AMS-IX provides exceptional network peering
- **Central position** — Better connectivity to Central and Eastern Europe than London
- **Strong infrastructure** — Major datacenter market with reliable availability
- **EU based** — Netherlands hosting for European data requirements

## Best For

- Central European users (Germany, Scandinavia, Benelux)
- Applications needing excellent European network peering
- Alternative to London for European deployments
- Spaces serving balanced pan-European audiences

## Latency

- Netherlands/Belgium: < 5ms
- Germany/Scandinavia: ~10-15ms
- UK/France: ~10-15ms
- US East Coast: ~85ms
