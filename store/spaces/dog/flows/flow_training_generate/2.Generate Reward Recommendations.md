# Reward Recommendations

## High-Value Treats
- Freeze-dried liver.
- Small pieces of plain boiled chicken.
- Small amounts of cheese (if dog is cheese-tolerant).

## Low-Value Treats
- Regular kibble.
- Commercial mini-treats.

## Non-Food Rewards
- Verbal praise ("Good boy/girl!").
- Head scratches and petting.
- Favorite toy or brief game of tug-of-war.
