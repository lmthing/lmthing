# Step 5: Finalize and Optimize

**Agent:** Cat Care Advisor

## Task

Compile all elements into a cohesive, actionable weekly care plan.

## Inputs

- Daily checklist (Step 2)
- Weekly schedule (Step 3)
- Enrichment activities (Step 4)
- Owner feedback/constraints

## Instructions

Create the final care routine document with:

### 1. Quick Reference Summary
- Time commitment per day (range)
- Weekly time investment
- Critical daily non-negotiables
- Flexible/optional activities

### 2. Complete Daily Checklist
- Merged with enrichment activities
- Time estimates for each task
- Priority indicators (must-do vs. ideal)

### 3. Weekly Calendar View
- All tasks laid out day-by-day
- Color-coded by category (feeding, grooming, play, health)
- Alternative day options for flexibility

### 4. Getting Started Tips
- How to transition to new routine
- What to track/monitor
- Adjustment period expectations
- When to reassess and update

### 5. Troubleshooting Guide
- What to do if you miss tasks
- How to handle schedule disruptions
- Signs your cat needs more/less of something

### 6. Next Review Date
- When to reassess routine (typically 2-4 weeks)
- What to observe and track

## Output

A comprehensive, user-friendly weekly care routine document that the owner can immediately implement. Format for easy printing or digital use.
