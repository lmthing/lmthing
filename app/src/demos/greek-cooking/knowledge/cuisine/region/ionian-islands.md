# Ionian Islands (Επτάνησα)

Corfu, Zakynthos, Kefalonia and the Ionian islands show strong Venetian and Italian influences from centuries of rule.

## Key Characteristics
- Italian-influenced techniques
- Garlic used more liberally
- Wine-based sauces
- Unique spice combinations
- Less Ottoman, more Western European influence

## Signature Dishes
- Sofrito (veal in garlic-wine sauce)
- Pastitsada (spiced meat stew with pasta)
- Bourdeto (spicy fish stew)
- Mandolato (nougat)
- Bianco (white fish soup)

## Pantry Essentials
- White wine
- Nutmeg and cloves
- Fresh parsley (more than other regions)
- Local olive oils
- Graviera cheese
