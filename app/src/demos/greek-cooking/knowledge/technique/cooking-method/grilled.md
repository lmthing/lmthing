# Grilled (Στα Κάρβουνα)

Charcoal grilling is beloved in Greece — for souvlaki, fish, and more.

## Technique Basics
- Charcoal preferred over gas
- High heat for souvlaki, moderate for fish
- Oil the food, not the grill
- Let rest before serving

## Classic Grilled Items
- Souvlaki (pork, chicken, lamb)
- Whole fish (lavraki, tsipoura)
- Lamb chops (paidakia)
- Biftekia (Greek burgers)
- Octopus
- Halloumi cheese
- Vegetables (peppers, eggplant)

## Greek Grilling Style
- Marinate with olive oil, lemon, oregano
- Don't over-flip — once is enough
- Squeeze lemon at the table
- Serve immediately

## Souvlaki Specifics
- Cut meat in 1-inch cubes
- Alternate with onion/pepper optional
- Grill hot and fast
- Rest briefly, serve in pita

## Equipment
- Charcoal grill (foukou)
- Metal skewers (souvles)
- Grill basket for fish
- Wire brush for cleaning
