# Advanced Musician

**Years:** 5+ years

## Key Characteristics
- Strong technical command of instrument/voice
- Advanced sight-reading abilities
- Significant ensemble and performance experience
- Beginning composition or arrangement interest

## Teaching Approach
- Focus on artistic interpretation and refinement
- Challenge with advanced repertoire and techniques
- Exploration of composer intent and historical context
- Mentorship and independent learning
