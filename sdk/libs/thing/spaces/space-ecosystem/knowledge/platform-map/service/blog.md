# lmthing.blog

A personalized AI news and research platform that synthesizes content from your RSS feeds and web searches into a continuously updated, AI-curated news feed.

## What It Does

Blog aggregates content from RSS feeds and web searches, then uses AI to synthesize, summarize, and present the most relevant information. You can trigger deep research on any topic and publish your own AI-synthesized stories to your public profile.

## Key Features

- **RSS feed management** — Subscribe to and manage content sources
- **AI synthesis** — Continuously updated feed with AI-curated summaries
- **Deep research** — On-demand deep dives into any topic
- **Publishing** — Share AI-synthesized stories publicly
- **Tiered access** — Free tier with limited feeds, $5/month for full access

## When to Use

Use Blog when you want to stay informed about AI developments, industry news, or any topic area. It's especially useful for research, trend analysis, and content curation. The deep research feature is valuable for exploring new topics in depth.

## How It Connects

Blog uses the same LLM proxy as other services for AI synthesis. Content is generated via the generate-ai edge function. Authentication follows the standard SSO flow through com/.
