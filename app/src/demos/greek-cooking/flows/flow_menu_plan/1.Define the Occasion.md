# Define the Occasion

The occasion shapes every menu decision in Greek cooking:

## Everyday Dining
- Simple, nourishing meals
- Focus on seasonal ingredients
- One main dish with bread and salad
- Quick preparation time

## Sunday Family Lunch
- The most important meal of the week
- Multiple courses served simultaneously
- Roasted meats or traditional stews
- Extended leisurely dining

## Name Day / Birthday
- Celebratory dishes
- Generous portions for guests
- Traditional sweets required
- Alcohol accompaniments

## Easter (Πάσχα)
- Most important holiday menu
- Lamb is essential
- Specific traditional dishes
- Weekend-long preparation

## Christmas / New Year
- Roast pork or turkey
- Melomakarona, kourabiedes required
- Vasilopita for New Year
- Abundant meze selection

## Summer Gathering
- Light, fresh dishes
- Grilled seafood and meats
- Cold meze and salads
- Fruit-based desserts
