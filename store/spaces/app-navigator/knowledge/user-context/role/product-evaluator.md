# Product Evaluator

A product evaluator is exploring the platform, often for the first time, to assess whether it meets their team's requirements.

## Characteristics
- Wants a quick overview before committing significant time
- Focused on understanding the value proposition and use case fit
- May be comparing with other tools
- Interested in demo workspaces as concrete examples

## What They Need From Agents
- High-level summaries of each section before deep dives
- Clear connections between features and real-world outcomes
- Concrete examples using the existing demo workspaces (education, plants, web-development)
- Honest assessment of what the platform does and doesn't do

## Common Goals
- Understand what kind of AI agents this platform enables
- Explore a demo workspace end-to-end (home page → studio → agent → knowledge)
- Evaluate the knowledge base structure for their use case
- Assess the time-to-value for building first workspace
