# Nistisimo (Νηστίσιμο) - Greek Fasting

Greek Orthodox fasting cuisine excludes meat, dairy, eggs, and sometimes oil and wine.

## Fasting Levels
- **Regular fasting**: No meat, dairy, eggs
- **Strict fasting**: Also no olive oil or wine
- **Shellfish allowed**: On most fasting days

## Traditional Fasting Dishes
- Fasolada (bean soup)
- Fakes (lentil soup)
- Revithia (chickpea stew)
- Dolmades with rice (no meat)
- Halvas semolina (no butter/eggs)
- Taramosalata (fish roe is permitted)

## Major Fasting Periods
- Great Lent (40 days before Easter)
- Advent (before Christmas)
- Dormition Fast (August)
- Apostles' Fast (after Pentecost)

## Tips
- These dishes are delicious year-round
- Focus on umami from legumes and tomatoes
- Tahini replaces dairy for creaminess
