# Ternary Form

## Definition
Three-part musical form (A-B-A) with return to opening material.

## Characteristics
- Opening section (A)
- Contrasting middle section (B)
- Return of opening section (A)
- provides satisfying closure

## Examples
- Song form
- Minuet and trio
- Simple lied forms
- Many popular songs

## Teaching Approach
- Natural and intuitive structure
- Common in many styles
- Good for understanding harmonic return
- Basis for more complex forms
