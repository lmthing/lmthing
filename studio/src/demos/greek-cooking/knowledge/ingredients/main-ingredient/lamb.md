# Lamb (Αρνί)

Lamb is the most celebrated meat in Greek cuisine, central to holidays and special occasions.

## Best Cuts
- **Leg (Μπούτι)**: Roasting, kleftiko
- **Shoulder (Σπάλα)**: Braising, slow-cooking
- **Chops (Παϊδάκια)**: Grilling
- **Ground (Κιμάς)**: Moussaka, keftedes

## Classic Preparations
- Arni kleftiko (parchment-roasted)
- Arni yiouvetsi (with orzo)
- Lamb souvlaki
- Whole roasted lamb (Easter)
- Arnaki fricassee (with lettuce and dill)

## Cooking Tips
- Bring to room temperature before cooking
- Greek lamb is smaller than American — adjust times
- Pair with lemon, oregano, and garlic
- Don't skip the fat — it's flavorful
- Rest well before cutting

## Sourcing
- Young lamb preferred (milk-fed best)
- Look for Greek or Mediterranean lamb if possible
- Spring lamb is most prized
