# Spring (Άνοιξη)

Spring brings tender new ingredients and the most important Greek holiday — Easter.

## Peak Ingredients
- Young lamb (milk-fed)
- Artichokes (aginares)
- Fresh broad beans (koukia)
- Wild greens at their best
- Fresh dill and fennel
- Spring onions
- Green almonds
- Fresh peas

## Seasonal Dishes
- Artichokes à la polita
- Lamb fricassee with lettuce
- Fresh fava beans with dill
- Magiritsa (Easter soup)
- Kokoretsi (Easter offal)
- Tsoureki (Easter bread)

## Easter (Πάσχα)
The biggest Greek celebration:
- Lamb is mandatory
- Red eggs (kokkina avga)
- Magiritsa on Saturday midnight
- Whole lamb on Sunday
- Kourabiedes and tsoureki

## Cooking Notes
- Vegetables are tender — shorter cooking times
- Fresh artichokes need lemon water to prevent browning
- Take advantage of wild horta abundance
