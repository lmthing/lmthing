# Choose Space to Deploy

Identify which space from the user's workspace should be deployed to a container.

Review the space's structure to ensure it's ready for deployment:
- Does it have a valid package.json with name and version?
- Are all agents complete with config.json, instruct.md, and values.json?
- Do all flows have index.md and numbered step files?
- Are knowledge domain and field configs valid?
- Do all cross-references resolve (flowIds, enabledKnowledgeFields, runtimeFields)?

The slug must be lowercase alphanumeric with hyphens only. It becomes part of the space's URL and must be unique across all lmthing spaces.
