# Band Ensemble

## Characteristics
- Woodwind, brass, and percussion ensemble
- Flexible instrumentation allows various ensemble sizes
- Bright, energetic sound profile

## Rehearsal Considerations
- Tuning and intonation across different sections
- Attention to balance in absence of string section
- Marching and concert band styles
- Extensive new music written for band
