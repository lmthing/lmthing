# Outdoor Grill (Ψησταριά)

Essential for authentic Greek grilled dishes — preferably charcoal.

## Ideal Setup
- Charcoal grill (preferred)
- Gas grill (acceptable)
- Multiple heat zones
- Lid for indirect cooking

## Greek Grilling Equipment
- Metal souvlaki skewers (flat, not round)
- Long tongs
- Fish grilling basket
- Basting brush
- Heat-resistant gloves

## Best For
- Souvlaki of all types
- Paidakia (lamb chops)
- Whole fish
- Biftekia
- Grilled halloumi
- Grilled vegetables
- Octopus

## Charcoal Tips
- Wait for white ash coating
- Create hot and cool zones
- Don't flip too often
- Rest meat off the grill

## Greek Tradition
Sunday grilling is a social event. The grill master (usually male, traditionally) tends the fire while others prepare sides and enjoy drinks.
