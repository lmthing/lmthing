# Europe Central — Amsterdam, Netherlands (AMS)

Located in Amsterdam, one of the world's largest internet exchange hubs with excellent connectivity to all of Europe.

## Why Choose AMS

- **Internet exchange hub** — Amsterdam Internet Exchange (AMS-IX) provides exceptional peering
- **Central location** — Better connectivity to Central and Eastern Europe than London
- **Strong infrastructure** — Major datacenter market with reliable availability
- **Alternative to LHR** — Slightly better latency to Germany, Scandinavia, and Eastern Europe

## Best For

- Users in Central Europe (Germany, Netherlands, Belgium, Scandinavia)
- Applications needing excellent European network peering
- Teams preferring Netherlands-based data hosting
- Users who want balanced European coverage

## Latency

- Netherlands/Belgium: < 5ms
- Germany/Scandinavia: ~10-15ms
- UK/France: ~10-15ms
- US East Coast: ~85ms
