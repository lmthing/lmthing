# Modal Harmony

## Definition
Harmonic progressions based on modal scales (Dorian, Phrygian, Lydian, Mixolydian, etc.).

## Key Elements
- Drones and repeated harmonic bases
- Different color from major/minor tonality
- Common in folk and contemporary music
- Ambiguous tonal center possibilities

## Teaching Approach
- Expands harmonic vocabulary beyond major/minor
- Common in jazz and world music
- Supports contemporary music analysis
- Enriches composition possibilities
