# Map Available Services

Walk through the services relevant to the user's goal. Don't cover all ten — focus on the 3-4 that matter most.

For each relevant service, explain:
- What it does in one sentence
- What tier it requires (free, Pro, Computer)
- How it fits into the user's workflow

Group services by workflow stage:
- **Create** — Studio (build agents), Chat (test conversations)
- **Deploy** — Space (containers), Computer (personal node)
- **Share** — Store (marketplace), Social (public), Team (private)
- **Manage** — Com (account), Blog (research), Casa (home)

Keep explanations brief and goal-oriented. The user doesn't need to know about every feature — just enough to understand which services to explore.
