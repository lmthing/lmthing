# Anthropic Models

Anthropic's Claude models excel at nuanced writing, careful reasoning, and following complex instructions.

## Available Models

- **anthropic/claude-opus-4** — Flagship model. Exceptional at complex reasoning, nuanced writing, and multi-step tasks. Highest quality but slowest and most expensive.
- **anthropic/claude-sonnet-4** — Balanced model. Strong reasoning with faster speed than Opus. Good default for agents that need quality without the cost of Opus.
- **anthropic/claude-3-5-haiku** — Fast and affordable. Available on free tier. Good for quick tasks, simple conversations, and high-volume use cases.

## Strengths

- **Nuanced writing** — Claude excels at natural, human-like text with appropriate tone and style
- **Careful reasoning** — Thinks through problems methodically, less likely to hallucinate
- **Long context** — Handles very long documents and conversations well
- **Instruction following** — Excellent at following complex, multi-part instructions precisely
- **Safety** — Strong built-in safety features and refusal of harmful requests

## When to Choose Anthropic

Choose Anthropic when you need high-quality writing, careful reasoning, or agents that handle sensitive topics well. Claude Sonnet is the best default for quality-focused agents. Use Opus for the most demanding tasks. Use Haiku for speed and cost efficiency.

## Naming Format

All Anthropic models use the `anthropic/` prefix: `anthropic/claude-opus-4`, `anthropic/claude-sonnet-4`, etc.
