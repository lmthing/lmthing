# Free Tier Pricing

The simplest pricing approach — your agent is completely free to download or use. No payment required, no barriers to adoption.

## Strategic Benefits

- **Maximum reach** — removes all friction from the download/access decision
- **Community building** — free agents attract users who become advocates
- **Reputation** — establishes you as a generous, credible creator
- **Lead generation** — free agents drive traffic to your profile and paid offerings
- **Feedback loop** — more users means more feedback, which improves your agents

## When to Choose Free

- You're new to the store and want to build a track record
- The agent serves an educational or community purpose
- You plan to offer a premium version later (free as lead generation)
- The agent is simple enough that charging would feel disproportionate
- You want to maximize adoption for network effects

## Revenue Impact

Zero direct revenue, but free agents have significant indirect value. Top store creators typically offer a mix of free and paid agents — the free ones drive visibility that benefits their paid listings. Think of free agents as marketing that also helps people.

## Tips

- Mention your paid agents in the free agent's description
- Use the free agent to demonstrate your quality and expertise
- Track downloads to understand demand for related paid agents
