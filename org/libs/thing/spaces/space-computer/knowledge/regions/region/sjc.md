# US West — San Jose, California (SJC)

Located in San Jose, California in the heart of Silicon Valley with strong connectivity to the US West Coast and Asia-Pacific.

## Why Choose SJC

- **West Coast proximity** — Lowest latency for users on the US West Coast
- **Asia-Pacific connectivity** — Better trans-Pacific links than East Coast regions
- **Silicon Valley** — Strong infrastructure and peering with major tech companies
- **Complementary to IAD** — Good alternative when IAD doesn't serve your geographic needs

## Best For

- Users on the US West Coast (California, Oregon, Washington)
- Applications serving Asia-Pacific users
- Teams based in Silicon Valley
- Users who need lower latency to Asian services

## Latency

- US West Coast: < 10ms
- US East Coast: ~60ms
- Japan/Korea: ~100-120ms
- Europe: ~140-160ms
