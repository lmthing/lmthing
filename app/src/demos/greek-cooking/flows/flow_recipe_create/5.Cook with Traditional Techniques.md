# Cook with Traditional Techniques

Apply authentic Greek cooking methods:

## Foundational Techniques

### Yiahni (Γιαχνί)
Slow-braised dishes in tomato sauce with olive oil. The base of many Greek stews.

### Ladero (Λαδερό)
Vegetables cooked generously in olive oil until meltingly tender. Not stir-fried — slow-cooked.

### Avgolemono (Αυγολέμονο)
Egg-lemon sauce: temper eggs with hot liquid, never boil after adding.

### Sto Fourno (Στο Φούρνο)
Oven-roasted with lemon, oregano, and olive oil. Low and slow.

## Heat Management
- Start aromatics in cold olive oil
- Build layers of flavor — don't rush the sofrito
- Add wine and let it evaporate
- Tomatoes go in after onions are soft

## The Greek "Secret"
- More olive oil than you think
- Fresh lemon at the end
- Let dishes rest before serving
