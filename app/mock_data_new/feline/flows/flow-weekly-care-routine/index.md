# Weekly Care Routine Flow

This flow generates a personalized weekly care routine for your cat based on their profile, needs, and your availability.

## Agent

Uses: **Cat Care Advisor**

## Steps

1. Assess cat's age, health status, and activity level
2. Review current care routine gaps
3. Generate daily care checklist
4. Create weekly maintenance schedule
5. Provide enrichment activity suggestions
6. Offer optimization tips based on owner availability

## Output

A comprehensive weekly care plan including:
- Daily feeding schedule
- Grooming tasks with frequency
- Play/exercise sessions
- Litter maintenance
- Medication reminders (if applicable)
- Weekly tasks (nail trimming, etc.)
- Enrichment activities
- Health monitoring checkpoints
