# Season and Adjust

Fine-tune your dish with proper Greek seasoning:

## Tasting Protocol
- Taste throughout cooking, not just at the end
- Check salt levels after adding feta (it's salty)
- Balance acidity with sweetness if needed

## Essential Seasonings

### Salt
- Season in layers during cooking
- Coarse sea salt for finishing
- Remember: olives and feta add salt

### Acid
- Fresh lemon juice is essential
- Add at the very end for brightness
- Red wine vinegar for specific dishes

### Herbs (Fresh vs Dried)
- **Fresh**: Dill, mint, parsley — add at end
- **Dried**: Oregano — can cook with dish
- **Bay leaves**: Remove before serving

## Final Adjustments
- Drizzle with fresh olive oil
- Add cracked pepper
- Taste and adjust salt/lemon balance
- Let flavors meld for a few minutes
