# Traditional Equipment

Specialty items for authentic Greek cooking experiences.

## Clay Cookware
- **Giouvetsi pot**: Clay pot for oven-baked dishes with orzo
- **Briki**: Small pot for Greek coffee
- **Saganaki pan**: Two-handled small frying pan

## Phyllo Tools
- Rolling pin (long, thin style)
- Pastry brush (for butter)
- Clean tablecloth (for stretching)

## Specialty Items
- Mortar and pestle (for skordalia)
- Cheese grater (for graviera, kefalotyri)
- Mandoline (for even vegetable slices)
- Meat grinder or attachment

## Traditional Baking
- Circular baking pans (for pies)
- Tapsi (large rectangular roasting pan)
- Clay bread dome (for village bread)

## Where to Find
- Greek specialty stores
- Mediterranean markets
- Online retailers
- Bring back from Greece!

## Worth the Investment
- Quality clay pots last generations
- Traditional briki makes better coffee
- Heavy tapsi distributes heat evenly
- Good mortar produces better texture
