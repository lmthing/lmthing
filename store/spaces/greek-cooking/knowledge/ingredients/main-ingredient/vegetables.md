# Vegetables (Λαχανικά)

In Greek cuisine, vegetables are often the main event, not just accompaniments.

## Star Vegetables
- **Eggplant (Μελιτζάνα)**: Moussaka, melitzanosalata, imam baildi
- **Tomatoes (Ντομάτες)**: Base of many sauces, stuffed
- **Zucchini (Κολοκύθια)**: Fritters, stuffed, in pies
- **Green Beans (Φασολάκια)**: Ladera dishes
- **Peppers (Πιπεριές)**: Stuffed, roasted, fried

## Ladera Philosophy
Vegetables slow-cooked in generous olive oil until meltingly tender. Not stir-fried or crisp-tender.

## Classic Preparations
- Gemista (stuffed tomatoes and peppers)
- Briam (roasted vegetable medley)
- Imam baildi (stuffed eggplant)
- Fasolakia (green beans in tomato)
- Bamies (okra in tomato sauce)
- Kolokithokeftedes (zucchini fritters)

## Wild Greens (Horta)
Essential to Greek diet:
- Foraged dandelion, chicory, wild spinach
- Boiled, dressed with olive oil and lemon
- Serve at room temperature
