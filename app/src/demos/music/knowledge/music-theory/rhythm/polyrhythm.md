# Polyrhythm

## Definition
Simultaneous use of contrasting rhythmic patterns and meters.

## Key Elements
- Different rhythmic schemes in different parts
- Creates complex interlocking patterns
- Common in world music traditions
- Expanded rhythmic vocabulary

## Teaching Approach
- Advanced rhythmic concept
- Develops independence and listening
- Supports contemporary and world music
- Enriches ensemble timbral possibilities
