# Chromatic Harmony

## Definition
Harmonic progressions incorporating chromatic alteration of diatonic chords and chromatic passing chords.

## Key Elements
- Secondary dominants (V/V, V/IV, etc.)
- Borrowed chords from parallel keys
- Chromatic passing chords
- Chromatic voice leading

## Teaching Approach
- Builds on diatonic foundation
- Extends harmonic palette
- Supports romantic and contemporary music
- Develops nuanced harmonic analysis
