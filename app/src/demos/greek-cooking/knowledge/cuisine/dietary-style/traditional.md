# Traditional Greek Cooking

Full range of traditional Greek cuisine without dietary restrictions.

## Includes
- All meats (lamb, pork, chicken, beef)
- Dairy products (feta, yogurt, cheese)
- Eggs
- Seafood
- All vegetables and legumes

## Typical Meals
Balanced meals following traditional patterns with protein, vegetables, and starches served family-style.

## Cooking Tips
- Use generous olive oil
- Don't rush the cooking process
- Serve dishes at proper temperature (many are room temp)
