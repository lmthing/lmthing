# US East — Ashburn, Virginia (IAD)

The default region for lmthing computers. Located on the US East Coast in Ashburn, Virginia, close to major cloud providers and internet exchanges.

## Why Choose IAD

- **Default region** — Most lmthing infrastructure runs in IAD, so deploying here minimizes inter-service latency
- **Excellent connectivity** — Major internet exchange point with strong peering to AWS, GCP, and Azure
- **Capacity** — Fly.io's largest US datacenter with reliable availability
- **All-around best** — If you have no specific geographic requirements, IAD is the safest choice

## Best For

- Users in the Americas (US, Canada, Latin America)
- Applications that interact heavily with other lmthing services
- General-purpose deployments without specific regional requirements
- First-time users who don't want to think about region selection

## Latency

- US East Coast: < 10ms
- US West Coast: ~60ms
- Europe: ~80-100ms
- Asia: ~150-200ms
