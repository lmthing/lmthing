# Northern Greece (Βόρεια Ελλάδα)

Thessaloniki and the wider Macedonia region feature robust, hearty cuisine with distinct Ottoman and Balkan influences.

## Key Characteristics
- Richer, spicier flavors
- More use of red pepper and paprika
- Substantial meat dishes
- Ottoman pastry influences
- Dairy and cheese emphasis

## Signature Dishes
- Bougatsa (cream-filled phyllo)
- Soutzoukakia (spiced meatballs)
- Gyros (originated here)
- Koulouri Thessalonikis
- Kavourmas (cured meat)

## Pantry Essentials
- Red pepper flakes (bukovo)
- Kasseri cheese
- Phyllo dough
- Smoked meats
- Trahanas (fermented grain)
