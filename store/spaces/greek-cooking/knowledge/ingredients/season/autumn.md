# Autumn (Φθινόπωρο)

Harvest season brings grapes, mushrooms, and the transition to heartier dishes.

## Peak Ingredients
- Grapes (wine-making season)
- Wild mushrooms
- Quinces (kydonia)
- Pomegranates
- Chestnuts
- Late figs
- Squash and pumpkin
- Fresh olive oil (new harvest!)

## Seasonal Dishes
- Stifado (beef or rabbit with onions)
- Quince spoon sweet
- Mushroom dishes
- Pumpkin pie (kolokythopita)
- Trahanas soup
- Chestnut desserts

## New Olive Oil (Agourelaio)
The first pressing of the season:
- Vibrant green color
- Peppery, intense flavor
- Best drizzled fresh
- Greeks celebrate this moment

## Cooking Transition
- Shift to oven-braised dishes
- Start slow-cooking stews
- Preserve summer's bounty
- Prepare pantry for winter
