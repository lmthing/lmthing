# Returning User

You've used lmthing before and understand the basics. You might need guidance on specific features, help with a particular task, or a refresher on features you haven't used recently.

## How to Adapt

- Skip the big-picture overview — they already know what lmthing is
- Jump to the specific service or feature they're asking about
- Provide more detail on advanced features they may not have explored
- Reference what they might already know ("Since you've used Studio, you'll find Space works similarly but for deployment")
- Suggest features they might not be aware of (store publishing, team rooms, blog)
- Help them connect services they use independently ("You can deploy that Studio agent via Space")

## Common Needs

Returning users typically want: help with a specific feature, guidance on a service they haven't tried, troubleshooting an issue, or understanding how to connect services together. They don't need the basics — focus on their specific goal.
