# Classical Period

**Years:** 1750-1820

## Characteristics
- Clear periodic structure and symmetry
- Sonata form development
- Emphasis on melody and accompaniment
- Balance and proportion

## Notable Composers
- Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart
- Ludwig van Beethoven
- Franz Joseph Haydn
- Franz Schubert

## Pedagogy Approaches
- Clarity of line and phrasing
- Understanding sonata form
- Classical articulation and dynamics
- Harmonic analysis study
