# Working Breeds

## Characteristics
- **Purpose:** Bred to perform tasks like guarding, pulling, or rescuing.
- **Traits:** Strong, intelligent, alert, and protective.
- **Needs:** High mental and physical stimulation.

## Examples
- Boxer, Doberman Pinscher, Rottweiler, Siberian Husky.
