# Stovetop Braised (Κατσαρόλα / Γιαχνί)

Slow-simmered dishes in sauce — the heart of Greek home cooking.

## Technique Basics
- Start with softened aromatics
- Build flavor in layers
- Low heat, long time
- Don't rush the process

## Yiahni Method
The foundational technique:
1. Sweat onions in olive oil until soft
2. Add garlic briefly
3. Add tomato paste, stir 1 minute
4. Add tomatoes and liquid
5. Add meat or vegetables
6. Simmer until very tender

## Classic Braised Dishes
- Keftedes (meatballs in sauce)
- Soutzoukakia (spiced meatballs)
- Beef stifado (with pearl onions)
- Fasolakia yiahni (green beans)
- Octopus stifado
- Moschari kokkinisto (beef in tomato)

## Tips
- Taste and adjust seasoning throughout
- Add water if reducing too fast
- Cinnamon and allspice are common
- Finish with fresh parsley

## Timing
Most braised dishes need 1-2 hours minimum. Better the next day.
