# Plate and Serve

Present your dish in the Greek tradition:

## Serving Style
Greeks serve family-style from shared platters. Individual plating is more modern.

## Traditional Presentation
- Use simple white or terracotta dishes
- Generous drizzle of olive oil on top
- Fresh herbs as garnish (not decorative — to be eaten)
- Lemon wedges on the side
- Crusty bread always accompanies the meal

## Temperature
- Many Greek dishes are served at room temperature
- Ladera are best not piping hot
- Grilled items should be served immediately
- Dips at cool room temperature

## Accompaniments
- Greek salad (no lettuce — tomato, cucumber, onion, feta)
- Tzatziki or yogurt
- Kalamata olives
- Fresh bread for sopping up sauces

## The Greek Table
- Serve everything at once (except dessert)
- Food goes in the center
- Encourage sharing and conversation
- No rushing — meals are for enjoying
