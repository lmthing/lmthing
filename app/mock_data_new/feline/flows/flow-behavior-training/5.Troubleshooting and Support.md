# Step 5: Troubleshooting and Support

**Agent:** Behavior Trainer

## Task

Provide ongoing support resources and troubleshooting guidance.

## Instructions

Create a comprehensive support guide:

### 1. Common Challenges and Solutions

**Progress has stalled:**
- Break down steps smaller
- Increase reinforcement value
- Check for hidden stressors
- Verify consistency in response

**Behavior is getting worse:**
- Ensure not accidentally reinforcing
- Check for medical issues
- Reduce stressors
- Simplify environmental setup
- Consider professional help

**Cat seems stressed by training:**
- Shorter sessions
- Easier criteria
- Higher value rewards
- More play time separate from training
- Check body language for subtle stress signs

**Owner inconsistency:**
- Simplify the plan
- Set phone reminders
- Enlist household help
- Focus on one behavior at a time
- Celebrate small wins to maintain motivation

### 2. Warning Signs

Stop training and consult a professional if:
- Aggression escalating
- Cat showing signs of fear/anxiety
- Behavior spreading to new contexts
- Medical symptoms appear
- Multiple behaviors emerging
- Owner feels unsafe

### 3. Success Maintenance

Once goal is reached:
- Continue reinforcing intermittently
- Don't stop management strategies too soon
- Watch for regression during stress
- Maintain enrichment level
- Annual refresher sessions

### 4. Resources

- Book recommendations for cat behavior
- Certified cat behaviorist directory
- Video tutorials for specific techniques
- Support communities
- When to involve veterinarian

### 5. Next Steps Planning

After this issue is resolved:
- What other training goals?
- Ongoing enrichment plan
- Maintenance schedule
- Long-term behavior wellness

## Output

A troubleshooting guide with:
- Quick reference problem-solution table
- Warning signs checklist
- Success maintenance plan
- Resource list
- Contact info for professional help
