# Simple Meter

## Definition
Meter where the beat divides into two parts (duple division).

## Key Elements
- Time signatures: 2/4, 3/4, 4/4, etc.
- Straightforward counting and conducting patterns
- Most common in Western music
- Clear metric pulse

## Teaching Approach
- Foundation for rhythmic understanding
- Basis for most popular music
- Clear and predictable patterns
- Easiest for beginning musicians
