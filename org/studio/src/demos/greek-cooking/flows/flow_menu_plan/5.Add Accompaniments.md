# Add Accompaniments

No Greek meal is complete without these accompaniments:

## Salads

### Greek Salad (Χωριάτικη)
The essential accompaniment:
- Tomato, cucumber, onion, green pepper
- Block of feta on top
- Kalamata olives, oregano
- Olive oil and red wine vinegar
- NO LETTUCE (unless you want mockery)

### Other Salads
- **Maroulosalata** — lettuce with dill and spring onion
- **Cabbage salad** — shredded, with lemon
- **Beet salad** — with garlic and vinegar
- **Politiki** — cabbage, carrot, peppers

## Starches

### Potatoes
- Lemon potatoes (oven-roasted)
- Fried potatoes
- Mashed potatoes (less common but loved)

### Rice/Pasta
- Pilafi — rice pilaf
- Orzo (kritharaki) — often baked with meat
- Hilopites — egg noodles

## Bread
- **Crusty village bread** — always present
- **Pita bread** — for souvlaki occasions
- **Lagana** — for Clean Monday

## Order More Than You Think
Greeks always have leftovers — it's expected and welcomed.
