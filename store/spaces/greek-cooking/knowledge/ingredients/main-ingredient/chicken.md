# Chicken (Κοτόπουλο)

Chicken is a staple protein in Greek cooking, prepared simply with robust Mediterranean flavors.

## Best Cuts
- **Whole bird**: Roasting, traditional
- **Thighs (Μπουτάκια)**: Braising, grilling
- **Breast (Στήθος)**: Souvlaki, quick dishes
- **Wings (Φτερούγες)**: Lemon-roasted

## Classic Preparations
- Kotopoulo lemonato (lemon chicken with potatoes)
- Chicken stifado (with pearl onions)
- Kotopoulo kokkinisto (in tomato sauce)
- Chicken souvlaki
- Kotosoupa avgolemono (chicken soup)

## Cooking Tips
- Dark meat holds up better to Greek slow-cooking
- Marinate with yogurt for tenderness
- Chicken with lemon and oregano is foolproof
- Skin-on for roasting, skinless for stews

## Avgolemono Soup
The classic Greek comfort dish:
- Whole chicken simmered with rice
- Finished with egg-lemon sauce
- Perfect for cold weather and recovery
