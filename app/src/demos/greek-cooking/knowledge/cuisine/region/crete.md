# Crete (Κρήτη)

Cretan cuisine is celebrated worldwide as the foundation of the Mediterranean diet, emphasizing wild greens, olive oil, and simple preparation.

## Key Characteristics
- Exceptionally high olive oil consumption
- Wild greens (horta) as staple
- Less meat, more legumes and vegetables
- Distinctive Cretan cheeses
- Ancient techniques preserved

## Signature Dishes
- Dakos (rusks with tomato)
- Kalitsounia (cheese pies)
- Antikristo (spit-roasted lamb)
- Chochlioi boubouristi (snails)
- Sfakiani pita (cheese pie with honey)

## Pantry Essentials
- Koroneiki olive oil (Cretan extra virgin)
- Rusks (paximadia)
- Mizithra and graviera cheese
- Wild honey
- Dried carob
