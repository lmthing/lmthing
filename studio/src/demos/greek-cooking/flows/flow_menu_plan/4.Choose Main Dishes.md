# Choose Main Dishes

Select main courses based on occasion and season:

## Meat Mains

### Lamb (Αρνί)
- **Kleftiko** — slow-roasted in parchment
- **Lamb yiouvetsi** — with orzo pasta
- **Lamb chops** — grilled with lemon-oregano
- **Whole roasted lamb** — Easter tradition

### Pork (Χοιρινό)
- **Tigania** — pan-fried with wine
- **Pork souvlaki** — skewered and grilled
- **Roast pork** — with potatoes

### Chicken (Κοτόπουλο)
- **Lemon chicken** — with potatoes
- **Chicken stifado** — with onions
- **Kotopoulo kokkinisto** — in tomato sauce

## Seafood Mains
- **Grilled whole fish** — lavraki, tsipoura
- **Octopus** — grilled or stewed with wine
- **Shrimp saganaki** — in tomato-feta sauce
- **Bakaliaros** — fried cod with skordalia

## Vegetarian Mains
- **Moussaka** (can make vegetarian)
- **Yemista** — stuffed tomatoes and peppers
- **Briam** — roasted vegetable medley
- **Gigantes plaki** — giant beans in tomato

## Balance Tips
- One protein-heavy main
- One vegetable or grain-based option
- Consider offering both meat and seafood for larger groups
