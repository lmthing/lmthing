# Standard Kitchen

Essential equipment found in any home kitchen — sufficient for most Greek cooking.

## Basic Equipment
- Chef's knife and paring knife
- Cutting boards
- Large stock pot
- Medium saucepan
- Large skillet/frying pan
- Baking sheets
- 9x13 baking dish
- Mixing bowls
- Colander

## Greek Essentials
- Heavy roasting pan (tapsi)
- Large deep skillet with lid
- Wooden spoons
- Kitchen towels (many!)
- Lemon squeezer

## What You Can Make
- All soups and stews
- Ladera dishes
- Simple roasts
- Pan-fried dishes
- Salads and dips
- Most baked dishes

## Nice to Have
- Food processor (for dips)
- Immersion blender
- Kitchen scale
- Instant-read thermometer

## Greek Kitchen Philosophy
Greek cooking doesn't require fancy equipment. Simple tools, quality ingredients, and patience are the keys.
