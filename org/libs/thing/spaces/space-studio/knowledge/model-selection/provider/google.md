# Google Models

Google's Gemini models offer large context windows and strong multimodal capabilities.

## Available Models

- **google/gemini-2.5-pro** — Flagship model with massive context window. Excellent for processing long documents, codebases, and complex research tasks.
- **google/gemini-2.5-flash** — Fast and cost-effective. Good for everyday tasks with quick response times. Strong multimodal support.

## Strengths

- **Large context windows** — Process significantly more text in a single conversation than other providers
- **Multimodal** — Native support for text, images, and other media types
- **Research tasks** — Excels at synthesizing information from large amounts of text
- **Speed** — Gemini Flash provides very fast response times
- **Cost efficiency** — Competitive pricing, especially for high-volume use cases

## When to Choose Google

Choose Google when you need to process long documents, work with large codebases, or handle multimodal inputs. Gemini Pro is ideal for research-heavy agents that need to analyze extensive content. Gemini Flash is great for high-volume, cost-sensitive applications.

## Naming Format

All Google models use the `google/` prefix: `google/gemini-2.5-pro`, `google/gemini-2.5-flash`.
