# Step 4: Veterinary Visit Schedule

**Agent:** Vet Visit Planner

## Task

Create a personalized preventive care schedule.

## Inputs

- Cat's age
- Current vaccination status
- Health conditions
- Last vet visit date
- Veterinarian recommendations

## Instructions

Generate a preventive care calendar:

### Annual/Biannual Wellness Exams

**Kittens (under 1 year):**
- Monthly until 4 months
- Every 3 months until 1 year
- Vaccination series
- Spay/neuter timing

**Adult cats (1-7 years):**
- Annual wellness exam
- Vaccines per vet protocol
- Dental check/cleaning as needed

**Mature/Senior cats (7-10 years):**
- Biannual exams
- Annual blood work
- Blood pressure check
- More frequent dental care

**Geriatric cats (10+ years):**
- Quarterly exams recommended
- Biannual blood work minimum
- Urinalysis
- Monitor for age-related conditions

### Vaccination Schedule

Based on cat's lifestyle and vet recommendations:
- FVRCP booster (1-3 years)
- Rabies (1-3 years, per law)
- FeLV if at risk
- Others per vet advice

### Preventive Care Tasks

**Monthly:**
- Flea/tick prevention
- Heartworm prevention (if prescribed)
- Weight check at home

**Every 3-6 months:**
- Nail trim (at home or vet)
- Dental treats/care

**Annually:**
- Professional dental cleaning (as needed)
- Fecal parasite check
- Blood pressure (senior cats)

**Every 1-2 years:**
- Full blood panel
- Urinalysis (especially senior cats)
- Thyroid check (mature/senior)

### Appointment Preparation Checklist

Before each vet visit:
- List any behavioral or health changes
- Bring recent symptom logs if applicable
- Note any questions
- Bring carrier treats
- Ensure cat is secure in carrier
- Bring insurance info if applicable

## Output

- Personalized preventive care calendar for next 12 months
- Appointment preparation checklist
- Vaccination schedule specific to this cat
- Reminder to schedule next appointment before leaving each visit
