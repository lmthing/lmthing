# Music Theory Fundamentals

## Topics Covered
- All key signatures and modulation
- Chord progressions and voice leading
- Harmonic analysis of simple to moderate complexity
- Melodic analysis and phrasing
- Basic form analysis (binary, ternary)

## Application Level
- Supports confident ensemble playing
- Enables arrangement and composition experimentation
- Allows harmonic analysis of most common repertoire
