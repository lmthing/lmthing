# High School

**Grade Range:** 9-12 (Ages 14-18)

## Key Characteristics
- Strong technical skills fully developed
- Sophisticated musical understanding possible
- Rehearsal time often limited
- College/career awareness beginning

## Repertoire Suggestions
- Complex and challenging literature
- Wide stylistic range
- Opportunities for interpretation and arrangement
- College audition-appropriate pieces
