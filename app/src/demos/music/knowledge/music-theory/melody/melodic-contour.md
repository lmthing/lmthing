# Melodic Contour

## Definition
The shape and overall direction of a melodic line created by the succession of pitches.

## Key Elements
- Arc shape (rise-peak-fall)
- Direction (ascending, descending, undulating)
- Range and tessitura
- Phrasing and breathing

## Teaching Approach
- Helps musicians shape phrases
- Creates coherent and memorable melodies
- Primary tool for musical expression
- Essential for interpretation
