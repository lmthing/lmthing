# Free Tier

The free tier gives you access to the lmthing platform with limited token budgets and basic models. No credit card required.

## What's Included

- **Basic models** — Access to cost-efficient models like gpt-4o-mini and claude-3-5-haiku
- **Limited token budget** — A monthly allowance of tokens for AI conversations
- **Studio access** — Full agent builder with all workspace features
- **Chat access** — Personal THING conversations with free-tier models
- **GitHub sync** — Workspace persistence via your private GitHub repo
- **Store browsing** — Download free agents from the marketplace

## Limitations

- No access to premium models (GPT-4o, Claude Opus, Gemini Pro)
- Token budget resets monthly — when exhausted, wait for next period or upgrade
- No Computer node access (requires Computer tier)
- Limited Blog access ($1/week allowance, limited RSS feeds)

## Who It's For

The free tier is ideal for exploring the platform, learning to build agents, and evaluating whether lmthing fits your needs. It provides enough capability to build and test agents without financial commitment.

## Upgrading

When you need more tokens, consider the Starter tier ($5/month) for a modest budget increase, or upgrade to higher tiers for premium models and a Computer node. Upgrade through the billing portal in com/ or via the create-checkout cloud endpoint.
