# Pure/Light Olive Oil

Refined olive oil suitable for high-heat cooking where extra virgin would burn.

## Characteristics
- Blend of refined and virgin olive oil
- Higher smoke point (~465°F / 240°C)
- Neutral flavor
- Lighter color

## Best Uses
- Deep frying
- High-heat sautéing  
- Baking (when neutral flavor needed)
- General cooking base

## Greek Frying Tradition
Greeks traditionally fry in olive oil (not vegetable oil):
- Fried fish and seafood
- Keftedes (meatballs)
- Fried potatoes
- Zucchini fritters

## Tips
- Can be reused 2-3 times if strained
- Still healthier than seed oils
- Combine with butter for flavor
- Finish fried dishes with extra virgin drizzle

## Note
Many Greeks use extra virgin for everything, including frying. The oil degrades but remains usable. Budget accordingly.
