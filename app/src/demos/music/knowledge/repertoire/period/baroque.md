# Baroque Period

**Years:** 1600-1750

## Characteristics
- Ornamental melody lines
- Figured bass and continuo accompaniment
- Terraced dynamics (no gradual crescendo)
- Counterpoint and fugue

## Notable Composers
- Johann Sebastian Bach
- George Frideric Handel
- Antonio Vivaldi
- Henry Purcell

## Pedagogy Approaches
- Emphasis on baroque articulation and ornamentation
- Understanding harmonic language of period
- Rhythmic flexibility
