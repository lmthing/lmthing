# Update Pricing Strategy

Evaluate whether the current pricing is optimal based on your competitive analysis and performance data.

**Signals to adjust up:**
- High conversion rate (>20%) suggests you may be underpriced
- Unique capabilities with no close competitors
- Strong reviews and word-of-mouth
- Enterprise or professional target audience

**Signals to adjust down:**
- Low conversion rate despite good traffic
- Competitors offer similar value at lower prices
- Target audience is price-sensitive (students, hobbyists)
- New listing needs initial momentum

**Consider model changes:**
- Source purchase → API access if you want recurring revenue
- API access → source purchase if hosting costs are too high
- Paid → free if reach matters more than revenue right now

Make one change at a time and measure the impact over 1-2 weeks before making additional adjustments. Document your rationale for each change so you can learn from what works.
