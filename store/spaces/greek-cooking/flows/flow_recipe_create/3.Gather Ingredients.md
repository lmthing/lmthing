# Gather Ingredients

Assemble all ingredients, prioritizing authentic Greek products:

## Essential Greek Pantry
- **Olive Oil**: Extra virgin Greek olive oil (koroneiki variety)
- **Feta**: P.D.O. Greek feta from sheep/goat milk
- **Herbs**: Fresh oregano (rigani), dill, mint, parsley
- **Spices**: Cinnamon, allspice, bay leaves, mastiha

## Fresh Ingredients
- **Produce**: Tomatoes, eggplant, zucchini, lemons
- **Aromatics**: Garlic, onions, spring onions
- **Proteins**: Quality meat, fresh fish, eggs

## Substitution Notes
- If Greek feta unavailable, use Bulgarian feta (not French)
- Fresh oregano can be replaced with dried Greek oregano
- Mastiha can be omitted or replaced with a drop of anise

Weigh and measure everything before starting.
