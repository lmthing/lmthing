# Step 4: Add Enrichment Activities

**Agent:** Cat Care Advisor

## Task

Suggest enrichment activities tailored to the cat's preferences and energy level.

## Inputs

- Cat's enrichment preferences
- Activity level and age
- Available space and resources
- Behavioral needs

## Instructions

Provide a varied enrichment menu:

### Daily Enrichment (5-15 minutes)
- Interactive play recommendations
- Food puzzles or slow feeders
- Training exercises (if cat is receptive)

### Rotating Weekly Activities
- Window bird watching setup
- Cardboard box forts
- Paper bag exploration
- New scent introduction (cat-safe herbs)
- Hunting games
- Puzzle feeders
- Catnip toys (if cat responsive)

### Environmental Enrichment
- Vertical space recommendations
- Hiding spots
- Scratching post placement
- Safe outdoor access (if applicable)

### Mental Stimulation
- Clicker training suggestions
- New trick ideas
- Scent work games

Consider:
- Cat's age and mobility
- Indoor vs. outdoor access
- Multi-cat household dynamics
- Owner's time and budget

## Output

A categorized enrichment menu with specific activity suggestions, frequency recommendations, and implementation tips.
