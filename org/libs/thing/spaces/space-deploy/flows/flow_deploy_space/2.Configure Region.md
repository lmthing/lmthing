# Configure Region

Select the best Fly.io region for deployment based on the user's needs:

- **IAD** (US East, default) — Best for Americas, lowest inter-service latency
- **LHR** (London) — Best for Europe, GDPR compliance
- **AMS** (Amsterdam) — Best for Central Europe, excellent peering
- **SJC** (US West) — Best for West Coast, Asia-Pacific connectivity

Consider: where are the space's users located? Does the space interact with other lmthing services (IAD is closest)? Are there data sovereignty requirements? When in doubt, use IAD.

Region cannot be changed after deployment — to switch regions, you must delete and redeploy.
