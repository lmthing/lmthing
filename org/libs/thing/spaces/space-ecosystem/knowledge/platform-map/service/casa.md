# lmthing.casa

Smart home control and self-learning automation through Home Assistant integration. THING connects to your Home Assistant instance and learns to automate your home over time.

## What It Does

Casa bridges THING with Home Assistant, giving your AI agent control over smart home devices and the ability to learn patterns from your usage. Over time, it develops personalized automations based on observed behavior.

## Key Features

- **Device control** — Control Home Assistant devices through natural language
- **Automation management** — Create and edit Home Assistant automations
- **Pattern learning** — AI learns your habits and suggests automations
- **Remote bridge** — Connect to your Home Assistant instance remotely via Computer node
- **Self-learning progress** — Track how well the AI understands your patterns

## When to Use

Use Casa if you have a Home Assistant setup and want AI-powered control and automation. Casa is especially valuable for users who find traditional Home Assistant automation complex — THING makes it conversational.

## How It Connects

Casa runs on your Computer node, which connects to your Home Assistant instance remotely. It uses the same LLM infrastructure for natural language understanding and automation generation.
