# Thematic Development

## Definition
Techniques for evolving and transforming musical themes throughout a composition.

## Key Elements
- Fragmentation and extension
- Variation and ornamentation
- Sequence and transposition
- Inversion and retrograde

## Teaching Approach
- Essential for sonata form understanding
- Supports composition and arrangement
- Develops analytical listening skills
- Creates sophisticated musical discourse
