# Beginning Musician

**Years:** 0-2 years

## Key Characteristics
- New to formal music study
- Building fundamental instrumental or vocal skills
- Introduction to music reading and notation
- Developing ear training abilities

## Teaching Approach
- Frequent encouragement and positive reinforcement
- Clear, step-by-step instruction
- Lots of repetition and scaffolding
- Focus on foundational technique
