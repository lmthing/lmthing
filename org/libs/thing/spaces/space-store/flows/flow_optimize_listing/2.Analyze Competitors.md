# Analyze Competitors

Look at similar agents on the store to understand the competitive landscape.

Compare across these dimensions:
- **Descriptions** — How do top performers structure and write their listings?
- **Pricing** — What do comparable agents charge? Are you priced too high or too low?
- **Distribution model** — Are competitors using the same model? Would switching help?
- **Reviews and downloads** — What do popular agents do well that you could adopt?

Identify gaps in the market that your agent could fill. Maybe no competitor has good documentation. Maybe they all lack previews. Maybe there's a price point no one is targeting.

Use competitive insights to differentiate — don't copy competitors, but learn from what works and find your unique angle.
