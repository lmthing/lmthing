# Winter (Χειμώνας)

Greek winter cooking is all about warmth, comfort, and holiday celebrations.

## Peak Ingredients
- Citrus (oranges, lemons, mandarins)
- Cabbage and leafy greens
- Root vegetables
- Dried legumes
- Preserved meats
- Fresh pork (slaughter season)
- Cauliflower
- Celery root

## Seasonal Dishes
- Avgolemono soup
- Fasolada (bean soup)
- Pork with celery (hirino me selino)
- Lahano dolmades (cabbage rolls)
- Portokalopita (orange cake)
- Karydopita (walnut cake)
- Christmas cookies (melomakarona, kourabiedes)

## Holiday Cooking
**Christmas (Χριστούγεννα)**
- Roast pork or turkey
- Christopsomo (Christmas bread)
- Melomakarona and kourabiedes mandatory

**New Year (Πρωτοχρονιά)**
- Vasilopita (coin cake)
- Pork continues
- Good luck foods

## Cooking Style
- Long braises and stews
- Oven dishes
- Warm soups
- Hearty legumes
- Preserved ingredients shine
