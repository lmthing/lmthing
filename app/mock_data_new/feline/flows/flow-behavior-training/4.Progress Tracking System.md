# Step 4: Progress Tracking System

**Agent:** Behavior Trainer

## Task

Establish a system to track progress and adjust the plan as needed.

## Instructions

Create tracking tools and success metrics:

### 1. Daily Tracking Log

Template for daily notes:
- Date and time
- Training sessions completed (number, duration)
- Behavior incidents (unwanted behavior occurrences)
- Successes (desired behavior occurrences)
- Cat's mood/energy level
- Any environmental factors (visitors, schedule changes)
- Reinforcers used and effectiveness

### 2. Progress Metrics

Define measurable indicators:
- Frequency reduction (for unwanted behaviors)
- Duration changes
- Intensity changes
- Success rate in training sessions
- Generalization to new contexts

### 3. Weekly Review Checklist

Every 7 days, assess:
- Overall trend (improving, stable, worsening)
- Environmental modifications working?
- Reinforcers still effective?
- Training protocol difficulty appropriate?
- Owner consistency level
- Need for plan adjustments

### 4. Milestone Markers

Set celebration points:
- 3 days of improvement
- 1 week of consistency
- First success in new context
- Reduction by 50%
- 14 days incident-free (if applicable)

### 5. Adjustment Triggers

When to modify the plan:
- No progress after 2 weeks
- Behavior worsening
- New behaviors emerging
- Cat showing stress signs
- Owner struggling with consistency

## Output

- Printable daily tracking log
- Weekly review checklist
- Progress graph template
- Milestone celebration list
- When to seek professional help criteria
