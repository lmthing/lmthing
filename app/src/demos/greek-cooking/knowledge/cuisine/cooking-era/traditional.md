# Traditional Greek Cooking (Παραδοσιακή)

Authentic recipes as prepared for generations by Greek grandmothers (yiayiades).

## Characteristics
- Time-honored techniques
- No shortcuts or modern conveniences
- Seasonal and local ingredients
- Recipe by taste, not measurement
- Stories and traditions attached

## Cooking Style
- Long, slow cooking times
- Wood-fired ovens when possible
- Handmade phyllo dough
- Fresh herbs from the garden
- Preserved foods for winter

## Learning Approach
- Respect the original recipe first
- Understand why each step exists
- Taste constantly as yiayia did
- Accept variation between families

## Common Phrases
- "Στο μάτι" (by eye) — measuring by intuition
- "Μέχρι να ροδίσει" — until it turns golden
- "Σιγά-σιγά" — slowly, slowly
