# Romantic Period

**Years:** 1820-1910

## Characteristics
- Emotional expression and virtuosity
- Expanded harmonic language
- Dynamic contrast and expression
- Rubato and interpretive freedom

## Notable Composers
- Felix Mendelssohn
- Frédéric Chopin
- Franz Liszt
- Richard Wagner

## Pedagogy Approaches
- Emotional communication and interpretation
- Vibrato and tonal control
- Understanding romantic harmony
- Development of personal artistic voice
