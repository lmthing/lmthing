# Prepare Mise en Place

Proper preparation is essential for Greek cooking success:

## Cutting Techniques
- **Dice onions fine** for sauces, larger for stews
- **Slice eggplant** consistently for even cooking
- **Mince garlic** at the last minute to preserve flavor
- **Chop herbs roughly** — Greeks prefer rustic cuts

## Pre-cooking Steps
- Salt eggplant slices and let drain (30 minutes)
- Soak dried beans overnight
- Bring meat to room temperature
- Drain and crumble feta as needed

## Organize Your Station
- Group ingredients by cooking stage
- Pre-measure olive oil (you'll use plenty)
- Prepare your cooking vessels
- Have serving dishes ready

## Equipment Check
- Heavy-bottomed pans for even heat
- Clay pots (γιουβέτσι) for traditional cooking
- Phyllo brush for pies
- Mortar and pestle for skordalia
