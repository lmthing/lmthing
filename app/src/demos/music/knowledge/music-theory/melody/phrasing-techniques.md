# Phrasing Techniques

## Definition
Methods of grouping notes into logical musical phrases for expression and interpretation.

## Key Elements
- Breath marks and punctuation
- Crescendo and diminuendo shaping
- Rubato and flexibility
- Articulation choices

## Teaching Approach
- Develops musical communication
- Teaches interpretation beyond notes
- Creates coherent musical statements
- Builds listening awareness
