# Step 1: Assess Current Situation

**Agent:** Cat Care Advisor

## Task

Analyze the cat's profile and current care routine to establish baseline.

## Inputs

From knowledge base:
- Cat age and breed
- Health conditions and special needs
- Current feeding schedule
- Exercise needs
- Grooming frequency
- Owner availability

## Instructions

Review all relevant cat profile and care routine information. Summarize:

1. **Cat's Life Stage & Needs:**
   - Age category and associated care requirements
   - Breed-specific considerations
   - Health conditions requiring attention
   - Activity/energy level

2. **Current Routine Assessment:**
   - What's working well
   - Potential gaps or areas for improvement
   - Time constraints and availability

3. **Priority Areas:**
   - Most important care activities for this specific cat
   - Time-sensitive needs (medications, health monitoring)
   - Enrichment opportunities

## Output

A summary assessment that will inform the daily and weekly schedule creation.
