# Advanced Level (Προχωρημένος)

For cooks ready to tackle complex Greek specialties.

## Characteristics
- Multi-day preparation
- Precise technique required
- Multiple advanced skills combined
- High stakes (expensive ingredients)
- Traditional mastery

## Advanced Dishes
- Handmade phyllo dough
- Galaktoboureko (custard in phyllo)
- Whole roasted lamb
- Kokoretsi (Easter offal)
- Handmade pasta (hilopites)
- Complex baklava
- Preserved foods (spoon sweets)
- Magiritsa (Easter soup)

## Skills Required
- Phyllo rolling and stretching
- Custard making
- Syrup temperatures
- Whole animal butchery
- Preservation techniques
- Perfect timing on proteins
- Multi-course orchestration

## Challenges
- Phyllo tears easily
- Custard can curdle
- Syrup crystallization
- Offal preparation
- Holiday meal timing

## The Yiayia Standard
These are dishes that take years to perfect. Expect to make them many times before mastery. Even grandmothers continue improving.
