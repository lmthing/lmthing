# Middle School

**Grade Range:** 6-8 (Ages 11-14)

## Key Characteristics
- Increased technical capacity
- Developing individual voice/instrument control
- Social awareness influences ensemble dynamics
- Wider emotional range

## Repertoire Suggestions
- Moderately complex arrangements
- Mix of traditional and contemporary styles
- Pieces with individual part challenge
- Themes relevant to this age
