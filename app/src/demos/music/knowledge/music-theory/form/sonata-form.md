# Sonata Form

## Definition
Large form structure with exposition, development, and recapitulation sections.

## Characteristics
- Exposition: Two contrasting themes in different keys
- Development: Themes developed and explored
- Recapitulation: Return of themes in home key
- Complex harmonic structure and modulation

## Examples
- Classical symphony movements
- Concerto movements
- Chamber music first movements

## Teaching Approach
- Most wichtig classical form
- Advanced concept requiring analysis skills
- Foundation for understanding harmonic drama
- Essential for classical period study
