# Europe West — London, United Kingdom (LHR)

London datacenter with excellent connectivity to Western Europe and transatlantic links.

## Why Choose LHR

- **European proximity** — Lowest latency for UK and Western European users
- **GDPR compliance** — Data stays in Europe for privacy-sensitive deployments
- **Strong connectivity** — London is a major internet hub with excellent global peering
- **Second most popular** — Well-tested region for lmthing deployments

## Best For

- European users and teams
- Spaces with data sovereignty requirements
- Deployments serving primarily European audiences
- Spaces where GDPR compliance matters

## Latency

- UK/Western Europe: < 10ms
- Central Europe: ~20-30ms
- US East Coast: ~80ms
- US West Coast: ~140ms
