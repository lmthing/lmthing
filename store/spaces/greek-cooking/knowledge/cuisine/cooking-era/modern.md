# Modern Greek Cuisine (Σύγχρονη)

Contemporary Greek cooking that honors tradition while embracing innovation and global techniques.

## Characteristics
- Traditional flavors, modern presentation
- Lighter preparations
- International techniques applied to Greek ingredients
- Restaurant-style plating
- Fusion elements thoughtfully incorporated

## Modern Approaches
- Deconstructed classics (moussaka reimagined)
- Smaller, refined portions
- Temperature contrasts
- Texture play
- Local ingredients showcased creatively

## Notable Influences
- Athens fine dining scene
- Greek diaspora chefs returning home
- Farm-to-table movement
- Molecular gastronomy (subtle)

## Balance Principle
Always maintain connection to Greek flavor profiles:
- Olive oil remains central
- Lemon brightness
- Fresh herbs
- Feta and yogurt
- Mediterranean vegetables
