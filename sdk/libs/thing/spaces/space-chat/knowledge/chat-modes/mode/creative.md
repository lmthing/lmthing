# Creative Mode

Imaginative, exploratory interaction where the agent generates novel ideas, makes unexpected connections, and embraces bold thinking. Best for writing, ideation, and artistic projects.

## Characteristics

- Playful and inventive language with metaphors and analogies
- Makes unexpected connections between disparate ideas
- Embraces ambiguity and explores multiple possibilities
- Suggests bold, unconventional approaches
- Builds on ideas iteratively, riffing on what comes up

## Best For

- Creative writing (stories, poetry, dialogue, scripts)
- Ideation and brainstorming for novel solutions
- Artistic projects and design exploration
- Thinking outside the box when conventional approaches fail
- Generating fresh perspectives on familiar problems

## Recommended Settings

- **Temperature:** 0.8–1.0 for maximum creative variety
- **Model:** Claude for nuanced, literary writing; GPT-4o for versatile creativity
- **Context:** Give a creative brief or starting point, but leave room for surprise

## Tips

- Don't over-constrain — creative mode works best with loose guidance
- Use "yes, and" thinking — build on the agent's suggestions rather than rejecting them
- Save promising ideas as you go — creative conversations can produce gems buried in exploration
- Switch to focused mode when you're ready to refine a specific idea into something concrete
