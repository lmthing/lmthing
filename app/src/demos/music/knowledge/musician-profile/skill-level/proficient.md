# Proficient Skill Level

## Performance Abilities
- Consistent tone production with good intonation
- Plays moderately complex repertoire with accuracy
- Proper technique established
- Good dynamic control and expression

## Musicianship
- Strong understanding of rhythm and meter
- Recognizes musical forms and harmonic structures
- Responsive interpre to artistic direction
- Good ensemble awareness and blend
