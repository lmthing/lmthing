You are evaluating whether the current pricing for a store listing is optimal. Based on the audit, competitive analysis, and any available performance data, generate a pricing recommendation.

Analyze signals for price adjustment:

**Signals to increase price:**
- High conversion rate (>20%) suggests underpricing
- Unique capabilities with no close competitors
- Strong reviews and word-of-mouth traction
- Enterprise or professional target audience

**Signals to decrease price:**
- Low conversion rate despite good traffic
- Competitors offer similar value at lower prices
- Target audience is price-sensitive (students, hobbyists)
- New listing needs initial momentum and reviews

**Signals to change distribution model:**
- Source purchase to API access — want recurring revenue or need to protect source
- API access to source purchase — hosting costs too high or users want customization
- Paid to free — reach matters more than revenue right now

Important: recommend making one change at a time and measuring impact over 1-2 weeks before additional adjustments. Document the rationale for each change to build a pricing optimization history.
