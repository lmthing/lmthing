# Beginner Level (Αρχάριος)

Perfect Greek dishes for those just starting their cooking journey.

## Characteristics
- Few ingredients
- Forgiving techniques
- 30 minutes or less active time
- Minimal knife work
- Hard to mess up

## Beginner-Friendly Dishes
- Greek salad (no cooking!)
- Tzatziki (mixing only)
- Horta (boiled greens with olive oil)
- Fakes (lentil soup)
- Greek omelette
- Grilled cheese (saganaki)
- Simple grilled chicken

## Skills to Learn
- Properly dressing a salad
- Salting eggplant
- Boiling and draining
- Basic knife cuts
- Timing pasta/rice

## Tips for Success
- Read recipe fully before starting
- Prep all ingredients first (mise en place)
- Taste as you go
- Use a timer
- Don't be afraid of olive oil

## Equipment Needed
- Good chef's knife
- Cutting board
- Large pot
- Skillet/frying pan
- Colander
