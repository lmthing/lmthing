# Syncopation

## Definition
Emphasis on unexpected or off-beat notes, creating rhythmic tension and interest.

## Key Elements
- Off-beat emphasis and accents
- Anticipation and delay of expected beats
- Common in jazz and contemporary music
- Creates rhythmic vitality

## Teaching Approach
- Develops rhythmic flexibility
- Essential for jazz and popular music
- Supports artistic expression
- Prevents rhythmic predictability
