# Mainland Greece

Traditional cooking from the Greek mainland, combining influences from various regions.

## Key Characteristics
- Generous use of olive oil
- Fresh herbs: oregano, thyme, bay leaves
- Lemon as essential flavoring
- Slow-cooked meat and vegetable dishes

## Signature Dishes
- Moussaka
- Pastitsio
- Lamb kleftiko
- Fassolada (bean soup)
- Kokoretsi

## Pantry Essentials
- Extra virgin olive oil (Peloponnese or Kalamata)
- Dried oregano (rigani)
- Feta cheese (PDO)
- Kalamata olives
- Honey (thyme or pine)
