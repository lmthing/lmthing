# Configure Pricing

For paid distribution models, set the right price based on value, market positioning, and audience willingness to pay.

**Source purchase pricing:** Consider complexity (number of agents, flows, knowledge domains), knowledge depth (curated vs generic), uniqueness (competitive landscape), and target audience (enterprise vs individual). Start slightly lower to build initial sales and reviews.

**API access markup:** Set a percentage on top of base token costs. Start moderate (50-100%) and adjust based on usage patterns. Higher markups work for agents with unique, hard-to-replicate capabilities.

**Free:** Skip this step — no pricing configuration needed.

Research competitor listings for similar agents. Price too high and you reduce conversions. Price too low and you undervalue your work, which can actually signal low quality to buyers.
