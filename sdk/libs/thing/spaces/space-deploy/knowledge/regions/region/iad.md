# US East — Ashburn, Virginia (IAD)

The default region for lmthing space deployments. Located on the US East Coast with the best connectivity to other lmthing services.

## Why Choose IAD

- **Default region** — Most lmthing infrastructure runs in IAD, minimizing inter-service latency
- **Excellent connectivity** — Major internet exchange with strong peering to all major cloud providers
- **Reliable capacity** — Well-provisioned K8s cluster with consistent availability
- **Best all-around** — If you have no specific geographic requirements, IAD is the safest choice

## Best For

- General-purpose deployments without specific regional needs
- Spaces that interact heavily with other lmthing services
- US-based teams and users
- First deployments when you're not sure which region to pick

## Latency

- US East Coast: < 10ms
- US West Coast: ~60ms
- Europe: ~80-100ms
- Asia: ~150-200ms

Choose IAD unless you have a specific reason to deploy elsewhere.
