# Compound Meter

## Definition
Meter where the beat divides into three parts (triple division).

## Key Elements
- Time signatures: 6/8, 9/8, 12/8, etc.
- Dotted note beat divisions
- Lilting, flowing feel
- Less common than simple meter

## Teaching Approach
- Extends rhythmic vocabulary
- Common in folk music
- Supports style and genre diversity
- Develops more sophisticated rhythmic sense
