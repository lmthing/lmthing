# Free Tier Models

Models available at no cost on the lmthing free tier. These are efficient, fast models suitable for everyday conversations and simple tasks.

## Available Models

- **openai/gpt-4o-mini** — OpenAI's efficient model. Fast responses, good general capability, reliable tool calling. The best all-around free-tier option.
- **anthropic/claude-3-5-haiku** — Anthropic's fast model. Quick responses with Claude's characteristic clarity. Good for writing and concise analysis.

## Strengths

- **Speed** — Both models respond quickly, enabling natural conversational flow
- **Cost efficiency** — Low token costs mean your free-tier budget lasts longer
- **Reliability** — Well-tested models with consistent behavior
- **General capability** — Handle most everyday tasks well: questions, writing, simple analysis

## Limitations

- Less capable at complex reasoning compared to premium models
- Shorter effective context windows
- May struggle with nuanced, multi-step tasks
- Less reliable on very specific or technical domains

## Making the Most of Free Models

- Write clear, specific prompts — free models benefit more from good prompting than premium models
- Break complex tasks into smaller steps
- Use focused mode for better results on specific questions
- Save premium model usage for tasks that genuinely need it

## When to Upgrade

Consider upgrading to Pro when: you consistently hit token limits, tasks require deeper reasoning, you need longer context windows, or you want access to the latest flagship models.
