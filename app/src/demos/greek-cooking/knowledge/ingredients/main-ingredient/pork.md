# Pork (Χοιρινό)

Pork is the most common everyday meat in Greek households, versatile and economical.

## Best Cuts
- **Tenderloin (Φιλέτο)**: Quick cooking, souvlaki
- **Shoulder (Σπάλα)**: Braising, slow cooking
- **Chops (Μπριζόλες)**: Grilling, pan-frying
- **Belly (Λαρδί)**: Crispy roast pork

## Classic Preparations
- Tigania (pan-fried with wine and peppers)
- Hirino me selino (pork with celery avgolemono)
- Pork souvlaki
- Christmas roast pork
- Cured pork products (syglino, apaki)

## Cooking Tips
- Don't overcook — modern pork can be slightly pink
- Wine deglazing is essential for pan dishes
- Complement with dried herbs and citrus
- Fat renders well for crispy results

## Regional Notes
- Apaki (smoked pork) from Crete
- Syglino from Mani peninsula
- Traditionally cured for winter months
