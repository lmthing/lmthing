# Step 2: Create Daily Care Checklist

**Agent:** Cat Care Advisor

## Task

Generate a personalized daily care checklist.

## Inputs

- Assessment from Step 1
- Owner's daily schedule and availability
- Cat's feeding preferences
- Litter maintenance schedule

## Instructions

Create a daily checklist organized by time of day:

### Morning Routine
- Feeding tasks
- Fresh water
- Litter check
- Quick health check
- Play session (if applicable)

### Midday (if owner available)
- Additional feeding (if multi-meal schedule)
- Enrichment check

### Evening Routine
- Feeding tasks
- Litter cleaning
- Play/exercise session
- Grooming (if scheduled)
- Medication (if applicable)
- Bonding time

### Before Bed
- Final litter check
- Ensure water is fresh
- Secure environment

## Output

A formatted daily checklist that can be printed or saved. Include approximate time requirements for each task.
