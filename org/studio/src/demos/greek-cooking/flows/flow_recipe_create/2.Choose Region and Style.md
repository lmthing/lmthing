# Choose Region and Style

Greek cuisine varies significantly by region. Select the style that fits your dish:

## Mainland Regions
- **Athens/Attica**: Urban fusion, diverse influences
- **Thessaloniki**: Ottoman influences, spicier flavors
- **Peloponnese**: Rustic dishes, olive oil-centric
- **Epirus**: Mountain cuisine, pies, dairy-rich

## Island Regions  
- **Crete**: Dakos, wild greens, distinct olive oil
- **Cyclades**: Simple, fresh seafood, local cheeses
- **Dodecanese**: Eastern Mediterranean influences
- **Ionian Islands**: Italian influences, sofrito, pastitsada

## Traditional vs Modern
- **Παραδοσιακό (Traditional)**: Authentic grandmother recipes
- **Σύγχρονο (Modern)**: Contemporary interpretations
