# Contemporary Period

**Years:** 1910-Present

## Characteristics
- Diverse compositional approaches
- Expanded instrumental techniques
- Varied harmonic language (atonal, modal, etc.)
- Experimental and innovative scores

## Notable Composers
- Aaron Copland
- Leonard Bernstein
- John Adams
- contemporary various world composers

## Pedagogy Approaches
- Openness to diverse styles
- Understanding unconventional notation
- Technical innovation and extended techniques
- Engagement with living composers
