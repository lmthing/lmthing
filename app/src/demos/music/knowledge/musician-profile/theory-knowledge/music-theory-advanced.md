# Music Theory Advanced

## Topics Covered
- Advanced harmonic analysis (chromatic harmony, secondary dominants)
- Counterpoint and compositional techniques
- Complex form analysis (sonata, rondo, variations)
- Contemporary compositional techniques
- Historical style analysis

## Application Level
- Supports sophisticated arrangement and composition
- Enables analysis of complex modern repertoire
- Prepares for music theory teaching and academic study
