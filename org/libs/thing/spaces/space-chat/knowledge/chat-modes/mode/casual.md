# Casual Mode

Relaxed, conversational interaction where the agent responds naturally without strict formatting. Best for brainstorming, general questions, and exploring ideas freely.

## Characteristics

- Natural, human-like language without bullet points or structured formatting
- The agent asks follow-up questions to understand your thinking
- Responses are exploratory rather than definitive
- Tangents and related ideas are welcome
- The agent shares relevant context and connections proactively

## Best For

- Brainstorming sessions and idea generation
- General questions about unfamiliar topics
- Learning through conversation and Socratic dialogue
- Creative exploration without a specific goal
- Casual problem-solving where the path isn't clear yet

## Recommended Settings

- **Temperature:** 0.7–0.8 for varied, natural responses
- **Model:** Any model works well in casual mode
- **Context:** Keep it light — no need for detailed system instructions

## Tips

- Don't worry about crafting the perfect prompt — casual mode thrives on natural conversation
- Follow tangents when they're interesting — you can always come back to the main topic
- Use casual mode as a warm-up before switching to focused mode for execution
