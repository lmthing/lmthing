# Diatonic Harmony

## Definition
Harmonic progressions using chords derived from a single major or minor scale.

## Key Elements
- I, IV, V, vi chord progressions
- Common progressions (I-IV-V-I, I-vi-IV-V)
- Clear voice leading principles
- Strong sense of harmonic function

## Teaching Approach
- Foundation for harmonic understanding
- Basis for most popular music
- Establishes voice leading principles
- Prepares for chromatic harmony
