# Free Distribution

Offer your agent completely free on the lmthing store. Free agents get maximum visibility and downloads, making this the best choice for building reputation and community presence.

## When to Use

- Open-source agents you want to share with the community
- Educational tools and learning resources
- Community contributions and public utilities
- Lead generation for premium offerings — offer a free "lite" version alongside paid agents
- Agents that benefit from widespread adoption and feedback

## How It Works

Users can download the full agent package (including all flows, knowledge domains, and configuration) directly into their workspace with one click. They get everything — the complete instruct.md, config.json, knowledge base, and flows — and can customize or extend the agent however they like.

## Strategic Value

Free distribution builds trust and community goodwill. Many successful store creators start with free agents to establish credibility before offering paid ones. More users means more feedback, which leads to better agents. Free agents also drive traffic to your creator profile, where users can discover your paid offerings.

## Considerations

- No direct revenue, but significant indirect value
- Higher download counts increase your store visibility
- Users may fork and modify your agent — embrace this as community contribution
- Consider including attribution in the agent's instruct.md
