# Binary Form

## Definition
Two-part musical form (A-B) with contrasting sections.

## Characteristics
- Two distinct sections
- First section often modulates to related key
- Second section returns to original key
- Common in baroque dance movements

## Examples
- Baroque minuets
- Simple marches
- Preludes and dances

## Teaching Approach
- Foundation for form understanding
- Clear and accessible structure
- Good starting point for analysis
