# Summer (Καλοκαίρι)

Greek summer means ripe tomatoes, fresh seafood, and long outdoor meals.

## Peak Ingredients
- Tomatoes at their best
- Eggplant (melitzana)
- Zucchini and its flowers
- Fresh figs
- Watermelon
- Fresh fish and seafood
- Peppers of all varieties
- Cucumbers
- Peaches and apricots

## Seasonal Dishes
- Greek salad (horiatiki) — peak season
- Gemista (stuffed vegetables)
- Briam (summer vegetable roast)
- Grilled fresh fish
- Melitzanosalata
- Kolokithokeftedes (zucchini fritters)
- Spoon sweets from summer fruit

## Cooking Style
- Lighter preparations
- Room temperature dishes (ladera)
- Grilling outdoors
- Fresh over cooked
- Stay hydrated!

## Entertaining
Summer evenings are made for:
- Meze on the veranda
- Ouzo and tsipouro
- Grilled seafood
- Fresh watermelon to finish
