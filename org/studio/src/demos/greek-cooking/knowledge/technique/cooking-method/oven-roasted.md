# Oven-Roasted (Στο Φούρνο)

The most common method in Greek home cooking — set it and forget it.

## Technique Basics
- Low and slow is typical (150-180°C / 300-350°F)
- Generous olive oil base
- Lemon and oregano finish
- Cover early, uncover to brown

## Classic Oven Dishes
- Whole roast lamb or chicken with potatoes
- Moussaka and pastitsio
- Yemista (stuffed vegetables)
- Yiouvetsi (meat with orzo)
- Briam (vegetable medley)
- Fish plaki

## Equipment
- Heavy roasting pan (tapsi)
- Clay pots for traditional cooking
- Parchment for kleftiko-style

## Greek Oven Wisdom
- Potatoes absorb meat juices beautifully
- Turn items midway for even cooking
- Baste with pan juices
- Rest meat before cutting
- Everything is better next day

## Temperature Guide
- Quick roast: 200°C / 400°F (40-60 min)
- Standard: 180°C / 350°F (1-2 hours)
- Low and slow: 150°C / 300°F (3+ hours)
