# Intermediate Level (Μέτριος)

The sweet spot for most authentic Greek home cooking.

## Characteristics
- Multiple components
- 1-2 hours total time
- Layered techniques
- Some timing coordination
- Building flavor profiles

## Intermediate Dishes
- Moussaka (layered casserole)
- Pastitsio (pasta bake)
- Gemista (stuffed vegetables)
- Lamb kleftiko
- Keftedes (meatballs)
- Spanakopita (using store phyllo)
- Most meat stews

## Skills Required
- Béchamel sauce
- Proper meat browning
- Sauce making
- Multiple burner management
- Vegetable prep techniques
- Basic sauce emulsion (avgolemono)

## Challenges to Master
- Timing multiple dishes
- Not overcooking proteins
- Seasoning in layers
- Proper resting
- Temperature control

## Growth Areas
Once comfortable, try:
- Homemade phyllo
- Complex layered dishes
- Whole roasted meats
- Multi-course meals
