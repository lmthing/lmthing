# Intermediate Difficulty

## Characteristics
- Varied rhythm including syncopation
- Extended range with some leaps
- Functional harmony with chromatic elements
- Moderate length works (2-5 minutes)

## Typical Skill Requirements
- Confident sight-reading
- Good rhythmic accuracy
- Technical facility in multiple positions/registers
- Ability to balance with other parts
